Our Mission

At Little Cuddles, our mission is to provide a safe, happy, caring, stimulating and secure environment for our children where everyone feels valued, included and respected. Our goal is to create a school where every child has the opportunity to learn and thrive, ensuring that no one is deprived of the chance to receive an education..

Our Vision

Our vision is to provide accessible education for all in a joyful learning environment, Creating globally competent, ethically high-performing international-minded citizens through top-tier education to achieve excellence. Hence we are “A Happiness Hub of Future Leading Lights”

Why Choose Us?

Strong Parent-Student Interaction

We prioritize effective communication between parents and students.

Passionate Teachers

Our team consists of dedicated and affectionate educators who are committed to their students' well-being.

Individualized Care

Every child receives personalized attention and is treated with love and care.

Individual Attention

We ensure that each student receives the individualized support they need to thrive.

Hygienic & Secured Environment

A hygienic and secured environment prioritizes cleanliness, with regular disinfection protocols, alongside strong access control measures and surveillance systems, ensuring the safety and well-being of all the children & staff.

Mindful Practice

Mindful activities for preschoolers enhance their understanding of emotions and surroundings using straightforward methods, nurturing their ability to regulate emotions and develop resilience within a supportive setting.

Synergistic Learning

Toddlers engage in synergistic learning through collaborative activities, where they interact with peers and adults, combining their efforts to explore, discover, and learn together, fostering social and cognitive development in a supportive environment.

Innovative & Integrated Curriculum

Preschoolers benefit from an innovative curriculum that seamlessly combines diverse subjects and activities, fostering holistic learning experiences that promote creativity, critical thinking, and interdisciplinary connections.

Our Curriculum

Our curriculum follows Early Years Foundation Stage (EYFS), nurturing holistic development through structured learning and play-based exploration, tailored to each child's needs."

Our Teaching Method

Drawing inspiration from Montessori, Waldorf, and Reggio Emilia, our is curriculum inclusive and child-centric curriculum ensuring every student feels loved and supported in their learning adventure."

Our Teachers

Our teachers at Little Cuddles are extensively trained, experienced, and adept at fostering a nurturing environment where every child feels valued and supported to flourish.

Partnership (PTSS)

At the Little Cuddles, the bond among parents, teachers , students, and the school is strong and interdependent. Each member plays a vital role in nurturing the child’s development.
